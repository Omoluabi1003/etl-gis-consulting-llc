Service

Spatial Strategy & Roadmapping

Co-create a pragmatic GIS strategy that sequences investments, de-risks compliance, and aligns executives around measurable outcomes.

Request a roadmap session Book time with the principal
  • Stakeholder-driven discovery and capability benchmarking
  • Clear governance model with decision rights and RACI
  • Investment case that ties benefits to operations and resilience

Service quick facts

  • Engagement length: 4–6 weeks
  • Outputs: Roadmap, budget model, operating model
  • Ideal for: Agencies planning modernization or cloud migration

Problem

Leaders need a prioritized GIS roadmap that reduces risk, wins budget approval, and keeps teams coordinated across departments.

Consulting Approach

We combine executive facilitation with evidence-based maturity assessments to design a sequence of projects your team can deliver.

Week 1

Discovery workshops

Interviews with business, IT, and field stakeholders to document objectives, constraints, and compliance needs.

Week 2

Capability assessment

Score current-state platforms, data quality, security posture, and team readiness using a transparent rubric.

Week 3–4

Target architecture & roadmap

Define the target state, dependency map, and phased releases with clear owner assignments and success metrics.

Week 5–6

Executive alignment

Facilitate steering presentation, budget narrative, and risk register so leadership can approve with confidence.

Deliverables

Every engagement provides ready-to-use materials your team can operationalize immediately.

Strategic roadmap

Milestone plan with dependencies, owners, and decision gates that align to budget cycles.

Operating & governance model

Charters, RACI, and intake workflows to keep priorities visible and accountable.

Investment case

Total cost of ownership, benefit hypotheses, and risk mitigations tailored to your leadership audience.

Expected Outcomes

Leaders leave with clarity on what to fund, when to deliver, and how to measure success.

  • Prioritized backlog tied to mission outcomes
  • Cross-department alignment on data governance and compliance
  • Budget-ready narrative that accelerates approval
  • Risk register and mitigation plan for critical dependencies

Timeline

Typical roadmap sprints span six weeks with remote workshops and weekly checkpoints.
