Service

Spatial Analytics Enablement

Deliver executive-ready dashboards, predictive models, and user adoption so teams can act on spatial intelligence every day.

Plan an analytics sprint Book time with the principal
  • Data pipelines that keep dashboards trusted and current
  • Storytelling and design systems tailored to decision-makers
  • Change enablement that increases adoption and literacy

Service quick facts

  • Engagement length: 4–8 weeks
  • Outputs: KPI definitions, dashboards, enablement kits
  • Ideal for: Operations centers, capital planning, equity analysis

Problem

Leaders often see fragmented dashboards, unclear KPIs, and low adoption—preventing analytics from informing daily decisions.

Consulting Approach

We connect quality data with intentional design and hands-on enablement to build confidence in every release.

Week 1

Metric definition

Facilitate KPI workshops to align definitions, data sources, and owners for each decision pathway.

Week 2–3

Data pipeline hardening

Establish validation rules, refresh cadences, and observability so teams trust every published view.

Week 4–6

Dashboard & model delivery

Design role-based views, spatial models, and accessibility-friendly interactions with iterative user testing.

Week 7–8

Adoption & training

Launch playbooks, office hours, and measurable adoption goals so usage sticks long after go-live.

Deliverables

Each release ships with the artifacts and training needed for sustainable analytics operations.

Analytics specification

Source-of-truth KPI definitions with lineage, refresh cadence, and calculation logic.

Production dashboards

Responsive, accessible dashboards and story maps tailored to executive and operational audiences.

Enablement toolkit

Video walk-throughs, job aids, and training sessions that accelerate adoption and self-service.

Expected Outcomes

Teams gain confidence in analytics while leadership gets measurable performance improvements.

  • Trusted KPIs with data quality SLAs
  • Higher adoption and reduced shadow data sets
  • Clear narrative for board, council, or public reporting
  • Repeatable release process with embedded governance

Timeline

Analytics enablement programs are structured in two-week sprints with measurable adoption checkpoints.
