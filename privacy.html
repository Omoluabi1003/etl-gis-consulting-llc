Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2024-11-01. This policy explains how ETL GIS Consulting LLC collects, uses, and safeguards information shared through our website.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you submit in consultation or playbook forms.
  • Organization, interest area, and request notes you provide.
  • Technical metadata including IP address, browser user agent, referrer, and page path.
  • UTM parameters provided in the URL for marketing attribution.

How we use information

  • Respond to consultation requests and provide requested resources.
  • Coordinate scheduling and follow-up communications.
  • Maintain audit logs for compliance and security purposes.
  • Improve site content and user experience with consented analytics.

Analytics consent

We use Plausible analytics only after you grant consent via the on-site banner. You can change your analytics preference at any time using the “Manage analytics preferences” link in the footer.

Data storage & processors

Consultation and playbook submissions are stored in a secure database and routed to our team through trusted providers.

Primary storage

Lead submissions are stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with access restricted to authorized personnel.

Email delivery

Notification and confirmation emails are sent via Resend using authenticated domains.

Retention

We retain inquiry data for up to 24 months unless a longer period is required for contractual or legal reasons.

Your choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your information by contacting us at contact@etl-gis.com.

Access and correction

We will respond to verified requests within 10 business days.

Opt-out requests

You can opt out of non-essential communications at any time by replying to a confirmation email or contacting us directly.

Security

We apply role-based access, logging, and least-privilege controls to protect data in transit and at rest.
